What We Make

Manufacturing Services

From fine precious metal work to fashion collections and completely bespoke creations — we bring your designs to life at any scale.

Gold & Silver Fine Jewelry

Rings, necklaces, bracelets, and earrings crafted in 9K, 14K, 18K, and 22K gold, as well as sterling and fine silver. We support both hallmarked and custom alloy requirements for international markets.

Fashion & Costume Jewelry

High-volume production of fashion-forward pieces in brass, copper, zinc alloy, and plated metals. Perfect for retail brands and designers seeking trend-responsive collections with consistent finish quality.

Custom & Bespoke Design

Full CAD design service, prototyping, and production for original collections and one-of-a-kind pieces. Ideal for independent designers, private labels, and luxury brands wanting exclusivity.

How It Works

From concept to delivery

A streamlined, transparent process designed for international partners — with clear communication at every stage.

01

Quote Request

Submit your requirements — sketches, CAD files, or references. We'll respond with detailed pricing within 48 hours.

02

Design & Approval

Our CAD team prepares technical drawings for your approval. Revisions are included until you're fully satisfied.

03

Sample Production

A physical wax or metal sample is created and shipped for your review before bulk production begins.

04

Bulk Manufacturing

Once approved, we proceed with full production. Regular photo updates are shared throughout the process.

05

QC & Export

Every piece passes strict quality control. We handle hallmarking, packaging, and international shipping logistics.
