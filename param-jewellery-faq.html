Jewelry Manufacturing FAQ

Everything you need to know about working with Param Jewellery — from design to delivery. Can't find your answer? Request a free quote and we'll respond within 48 hours.

What does Param Jewellery manufacture? ✦
Param Jewellery manufactures fine gold and silver jewelry, fashion and costume jewelry, and fully custom bespoke pieces. We produce rings, necklaces, bracelets, earrings, pendants, and complete jewelry collections for international brands, designers, and retailers across 40+ countries.
Is Param Jewellery a reliable jewelry manufacturer in India for international brands? ✦
Yes. Param Jewellery is a trusted fine jewelry manufacturer based in India with over 15 years of experience. We have served clients in 40+ countries and produced 500,000+ pieces. We are ISO 9001 certified, RJC (Responsible Jewellery Council) compliant, and conflict-free diamond certified — meeting the compliance requirements of markets in the USA, UK, EU, and UAE.
What gold karats does Param Jewellery work with? ✦
We manufacture jewelry in 9K, 14K, 18K, and 22K yellow gold, white gold, and rose gold. We also work with sterling silver (925), fine silver (999), platinum (upon request), and base metals including brass, copper, and zinc alloy for fashion jewelry. We support custom alloy requirements for specific international markets.
What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ) at Param Jewellery? ✦
Our minimum order quantities are flexible to suit all business sizes:

Fine Jewelry: 10 pieces per style
Fashion & Costume Jewelry: 50 pieces per style
Custom Bespoke: 1 piece (no minimum)

Our monthly production capacity is 50,000+ pieces, so we can scale with your brand's growth.
How long does jewelry manufacturing take? ✦
Timelines vary by order type:

Sampling / Prototyping: 2–3 weeks
Bulk Production: 4–8 weeks

We provide regular photo updates throughout production so you're never left wondering about your order's progress.
Does Param Jewellery ship internationally? ✦
Yes. We ship to 40+ countries via DHL, FedEx, and bonded carriers. We handle full export documentation, insurance, hallmarking, and customs support. We support both DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) and FOB (Free on Board) shipping terms, depending on your preference and destination country.
Which countries does Param Jewellery ship to? ✦
We ship jewelry to the United States, United Kingdom, United Arab Emirates, Australia, Canada, Germany, France, the Netherlands, Singapore, Hong Kong, and 30+ other countries. We have hands-on experience with customs regulations, hallmarking requirements, and import duties in all major jewelry markets.
What gemstones does Param Jewellery use? ✦
We work with a wide range of gemstones including: natural diamonds (GIA certified), lab-grown diamonds, rubies, emeralds, sapphires, semi-precious gemstones, cubic zirconia (CZ), synthetic stones, freshwater pearls, and South Sea pearls. Custom stone sourcing is also available upon request.
Can Param Jewellery manufacture jewelry from my own design? ✦
Absolutely. We offer a complete custom and bespoke design service. You can submit your own sketches, CAD files, or reference images. Our in-house CAD team prepares technical drawings for your approval, and revisions are included until you are fully satisfied. We sign NDAs before reviewing any designs to protect your intellectual property at every stage.
Does Param Jewellery offer private label jewelry manufacturing? ✦
Yes. We offer full private label manufacturing and packaging for jewelry brands. This includes custom packaging design, brand labeling, and production under your brand identity. We serve independent designers, retail chains, and luxury brands seeking confidential OEM manufacturing partnerships.
What certifications does Param Jewellery hold? ✦
Param Jewellery holds the following certifications and compliance standards:

ISO 9001 Quality Management
BIS & Hallmarking Compliant
RJC (Responsible Jewellery Council) Member
Conflict-Free Diamonds (Kimberley Process certified)
REACH & EU Nickel Compliance
Custom compliance documentation available on request
How do I request a quote from Param Jewellery? ✦
You can request a free manufacturing quote at paramjewellery.com. Submit your requirements — sketches, CAD files, or reference images — and we'll respond with detailed pricing within 48 business hours. No commitment is required and all project sizes are welcome. An NDA is available before any file sharing.
What payment methods does Param Jewellery accept? ✦
We accept international wire transfer (T/T), Letter of Credit (LC), and PayPal. We offer flexible deposit structures for established clients and long-term partners. All payment terms are discussed and confirmed before production begins.
Is my jewelry design kept confidential at Param Jewellery? ✦
Yes. We take intellectual property protection very seriously. We sign a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) before reviewing any design files or proprietary information. Your original designs, molds, and collection concepts remain exclusively yours throughout and after the manufacturing process. We never share, copy, or reuse client designs.
What is the price range for jewelry manufacturing at Param Jewellery? ✦
Pricing depends on the type of metal, gemstones, design complexity, and order quantity. We accept projects with budgets from under $1,000 for single bespoke pieces to $100,000+ for large wholesale orders. All quotes are completely free and provided within 48 hours of submitting your requirements.
Does Param Jewellery manufacture fashion and costume jewelry? ✦
Yes. We manufacture high-volume fashion and costume jewelry in brass, copper, zinc alloy, and plated metals (gold-plated, rhodium-plated, silver-plated). This is ideal for retail fashion brands seeking trend-responsive collections with consistent quality. MOQ for fashion jewelry is 50 pieces per style.
Where is Param Jewellery located? ✦
Param Jewellery is a jewelry manufacturer based in India — one of the world's leading jewelry manufacturing hubs, known for skilled craftsmanship, competitive pricing, and a strong export infrastructure. We serve international clients across North America, Europe, the Middle East, and Asia-Pacific from our manufacturing facility in India.
